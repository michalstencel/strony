FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who are you?

We’re a boutique creative studio brandingi.pl - designing complete visual systems in a sprint model: fast, focused, and free of unnecessary formalities.

Who is this service for?

For small and mid-sized businesses, startups, and freelancers who need a cohesive brand identity - done quickly, clearly, and without corporate fluff. If you care about results, not red tape, you’re in the right place.

Do I need to have a logo idea?

Not at all. You can come with a rough direction or a blank page - together we’ll define your goals, values, and visual tone before diving into design.

How soon can we start?

We usually have 1–2 open slots per month. Once we connect, we’ll confirm your start date - often within just a few days.

What if I need a different project?

We can design other materials too: business cards, folders, banners, or landing pages. Just tell us what you need - we’ll send a quote or recommend someone we trust.

How can I reach you

The easiest way is through the form on our website or by email: hello@brandingi.pl. We reply quickly and directly, no endless forms or automated loops.

More questions?

From premium print materials to websites and automated online stores - you can create it all with us, in one place.
