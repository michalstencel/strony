FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Webflow?

Webflow is a platform that allows you to visually create websites without coding — while still generating clean HTML and CSS underneath. Some advanced features may still require technical support.

How much does a website cost?

It depends on the project’s complexity, but most range between €1100-€6500. Smaller landing pages cost less; larger CMS or e-commerce projects cost more. We always break pricing down by stage and scope.

How long does it take to build a website?

Small sites: usually 2-4 weeks. Larger ones with custom functions or animations take longer - timelines are set individually. A simple one-page can be done in as little as 7 days, while a complex multi-page CMS project naturally takes more time. The key factor is how quickly we get feedback and materials.

Can I edit the website myself?

Yes. More advanced websites include a CMS, letting you update text, images, and layouts easily - no coding needed. You’ll also receive a short guide.

Will my website work on all devices?

Yes. Every project is fully responsive (desktop, tablet, and mobile).

Are websites optimized for SEO and performance?

Yes. We make sure your site is structured properly, loads fast, and connects with Google Analytics or tracking tags. We can also help with content and keywords if needed.

Do you also handle integrations?

Yes. We implement everything from newsletter signups and forms to CRM systems, automations, payments, and e-commerce. Each integration is chosen to fit the project - nothing unnecessary.

Do I need to have a ready design before we start?

No - and honestly, we prefer to design everything ourselves. That way we can control the quality, UX, and how the final site performs. If you already have a design, we can review it, but we usually build from scratch for a more cohesive result.

What kind of clients do you work with?

We collaborate with everyone - from solo founders to large organizations. What matters most is the quality of the project, not the company size.

What if I need help after the site is launched?

We offer ongoing support - through a subscription or hourly basis. You can always come back later for updates or further development.
